#+begin_verse
A hug so deep,
resting on him the Christ,
isn't just etched
unto flesh of my chest,
but my heart bathes,
in the beauty of His love.

A close embrace,
not a speck moves,
the winds stand still,
while my mind soars,
above the skies,
across the blues,
within and without.
#+end_verse
